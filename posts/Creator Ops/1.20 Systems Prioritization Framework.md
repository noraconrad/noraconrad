Now that you have an understanding of your current operational level from the diagnostic questions, it's time to deicide where to focus your improvement efforts. We cannot do everything in one week - so let's find the most important areas and begin there.

The Systems Prioritization Framework will help you make strategic decisions about which systems to optimize first, to make sure you invest your limited time and energy in improvements that will deliver the greatest return on investment (both financially and for your mental wellbeing).
