### Automated Response System

#### Response Template Structure

```
INQUIRY TYPE: [Category of question]

TRIGGER KEYWORDS: [Words that indicate this response is needed]

STANDARD RESPONSE: [Complete email template]

FOLLOW-UP ACTIONS: [What happens after this response is sent]

ESCALATION CRITERIA: [When to involve you personally]

RELATED RESOURCES: [Links to portfolio, case studies, etc.]
```

#### Example: Pricing Inquiry Automation

```
INQUIRY TYPE: Pricing Request

TRIGGER KEYWORDS: rates, pricing, cost, budget, how much, price

STANDARD RESPONSE:
[Use "Q: What are your rates?" response template from FAQ above]

FOLLOW-UP ACTIONS: 
- Add to CRM with "pricing inquiry" tag
- Send calendar reminder if no response in 3 days
- Schedule follow-up email in 7 days if no meeting booked

ESCALATION CRITERIA: 
- Budget over $[AMOUNT]
- Existing client referral
- Time-sensitive project
- Potential partnership

RELATED RESOURCES:
- Pricing page: [LINK]
- Service comparison: [LINK]
- Case studies: [LINK]
- Testimonials: [LINK]
```
