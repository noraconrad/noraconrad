## Building Scalable Systems

### Level 1: Personal Content Creation

- You create all content personally
- Systems help you be more efficient
- Batch creation and automation handle distribution
- Time: 4-6 hours/week

### Level 2: Assisted Content Creation

- Virtual assistant helps with research and scheduling
- You create main content, they handle repurposing
- Templates and systems enable delegation
- Time: 2-3 hours/week (you)

### Level 3: Scaled Content Production

- Team members create content using your systems
- You focus on strategy and high-level content
- Multiple creators following established frameworks
- Time: 1-2 hours/week (strategic oversight)
