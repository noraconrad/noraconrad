### Step 1: Customize the Content Database

1. Navigate to the Content Database in your Operations Hub
2. Review the existing properties and modify according to your needs:
    - Update content types to match your platforms (blog, video, podcast, etc.)
    - Customize status options for your specific workflow (e.g., add "Scripting" for video content)
3. Add any additional properties relevant to your content strategy:
    - Target Keyword (Text)
    - Content Pillar (Select)
    - Monetization Method (Select)
4. Create templates for your common content types:
    - Click "+ New" then "New template"
    - Create templates for each content type with pre-filled information
    - Include checklists for standard processes

### Step 2: Customize the Task Database

1. Navigate to the Task Database
2. Modify task categories to match your business needs:
    - Content Creation
    - Audience Growth
    - Monetization
    - Administration
3. Configure priority levels that make sense for your workflow
4. Add any additional properties:
    - Time Estimate (Number)
    - Energy Level Required (Select: Low, Medium, High)
    - Delegated To (Person)
5. Create templates for recurring tasks:
    - Weekly content planning
    - Platform maintenance
    - Newsletter preparation

### Step 3: Customize the Metrics Database

1. Navigate to the Metrics Database
2. Update metrics categories to match your platforms and revenue streams
3. Add specific metrics you want to track:
    - Platform-specific metrics (views, engagement, followers)
    - Revenue metrics (sponsorships, product sales, affiliates)
    - Email list growth
4. Create weekly and monthly tracking templates

### Step 4: Customize the Finance Database

1. Navigate to the Finance Database
2. Adjust income categories to match your revenue streams
3. Update expense categories for your specific business costs
4. Add any additional properties needed:
    - Tax Category (Select)
    - Payment Status (Select)
    - Receipt/Documentation (Files & Media)
