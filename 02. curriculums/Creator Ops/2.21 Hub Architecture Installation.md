### Step 1: Duplicate the Operations Hub Template

1. Access the Operations Hub template link from the files section of this lesson
2. Click the "Duplicate" button in the top-right corner
3. Select your workspace as the destination
4. Wait for the duplication process to complete

### Step 2: Explore the Template Structure

1. Read through the template overview page
2. Click through each main section to understand its purpose:
    - Dashboard
    - Content System
    - Task Management
    - Business Metrics
    - Resources

### Step 3: Customize Your Sidebar

1. Identify the pages you'll access most frequently
2. Drag these pages to the top of your sidebar
3. Create dividers between sections by adding pages with emoji titles (e.g., "—— Content ——")
4. Consider organizing with these sections:
    - Daily Use (Dashboard, Quick Capture)
    - Content Management
    - Business Operations
    - Resources & Archives

### Step 4: Update Profile and Settings

1. Click on your profile picture in the top-right corner
2. Select "My Settings"
3. Update your name and profile picture
4. Set your timezone correctly for accurate due dates
5. Configure notification preferences based on your workflow
