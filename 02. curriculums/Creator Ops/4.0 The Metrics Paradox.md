### Moving Beyond Vanity Metrics

Before we build your metrics systems, let's address a critical mindset shift:

**Vanity metrics** feel good but don't drive decisions. They include:

- Total followers/subscribers (without context)
- Video views without engagement rates
- Email list size without open rates
- Social media impressions without conversions

**Decision metrics** directly inform what you should do next:

- Content engagement patterns by topic or format
- Conversion rates from different traffic sources
- Revenue attribution by content piece
- Growth trajectory rather than absolute numbers

Our goal is to build systems that track metrics that actually matter for your specific business model.
