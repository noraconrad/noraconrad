# Nora Conrad

**Status:** Active  
**External Site:** [noraconrad.com](https://noraconrad.com)

## Description

Operations for small businesses

## Details

A decade-long digital brand and content platform started as a personal blog in 2012 around planning, productivity systems, and entrepreneurship. Evolved into a multi-platform presence with 100K+ monthly blog views, an 8K-subscriber YouTube channel, multiple revenue streams (sponsorships, digital products, affiliate), and a community of work-from-home professionals.

## Technologies

- WordPress/Blog Platform
- YouTube
- Shopify
- Google Sheets
- Email Marketing
- Social Media Management

## Features

- Blog with consistent 100K+ monthly views across planning, productivity, and business content
- YouTube channel (@NoraConrad) with regular publishing and engaged audience
- Digital product suite (planners, templates, courses)
- Multi-platform content strategy (blog, YouTube, email, social)
- Sponsor integrations (Sunsama, Tella, Setapp)
- Revenue tracking and analytics across all streams

## Challenges

Scaling content production while maintaining quality; managing multiple revenue streams; sustaining audience engagement across platforms; pivoting brand focus as personal direction evolved.

## Outcomes

Built sustainable creator income; demonstrated ability to build and manage digital communities; created framework for content-to-revenue; established authority in productivity and planning space.
