Even if you've used Notion before, understanding its fundamental structure from a creator perspective will ensure you build a system that scales with your business.

### Step 1: Create Your Notion Account

1. Go to [notion.so](http://notion.so/) and sign up for a free account if you don't already have one
2. Verify your email address and complete the initial setup
3. When prompted to create your first workspace, name it something relevant to your creator business (e.g., "Your Name Creator Hub" or "Your Brand Operations")

### Step 2: Customize Your Workspace

1. Click on the workspace name in the top-left corner
2. Select "Settings & Members" from the dropdown menu
3. Upload a profile picture or logo for your workspace
4. Choose a workspace icon that represents your brand
5. Set your preferred appearance (Light/Dark mode) under "My Settings"

### Step 3: Familiarize Yourself with Notion's Building Blocks

1. Create a new page by clicking "+ New Page" in the sidebar
2. Practice creating these essential blocks by typing / followed by:
    - Text blocks (default when you start typing)
    - Headings (H1, H2, H3) for organizing content
    - Bulleted, numbered, and to-do lists
    - Images and files by uploading from your computer
    - Tables and databases for structured information

### Step 4: Learn Essential Navigation Shortcuts

1. Quick Find: Press Cmd/Ctrl + P to search for any page or content
2. Slash Commands: Type / to access all blocks and functions
3. Try these helpful keyboard shortcuts:
    - Cmd/Ctrl + B for bold text
    - Cmd/Ctrl + I for italic text
    - Cmd/Ctrl + K to insert a link
    - Cmd/Ctrl + Shift + M to comment

### Step 5: Set Up the Mobile App

1. Download the Notion app from the App Store or Google Play
2. Log in with your account credentials
3. Enable notifications for important updates
4. Test creating a quick note on mobile to ensure it syncs to your desktop
