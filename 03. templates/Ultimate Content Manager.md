**Notion Content & Social Media Planner Template**  
Perfect for content creators and small business owners who want to streamline their content planning, execution, and social media campaigns—all in one place.

**✔ Partners Database**  
Easily track affiliates, sponsors, and partnered content to maximize your income across platforms.

**✔ All Content Database**  
Stay organized with a complete overview of your content—filter by status, channel, and quickly update or brainstorm new ideas.

**✔ Library Database**  
Build a central hub for all your B-roll, images, and brand assets, making content creation seamless and efficient.

Whether you’re focused on one platform or running multiple, track and link content across unlimited channels (YouTube, Instagram, TikTok, etc.).

Instantly see your content calendar, schedule new ideas, and edit directly within the calendar for a smooth workflow.

**✔ Extra Templates for Social Media Content**  
Ready-made templates to help you execute and schedule your social media content effortlessly.

Align your content with your business goals and plan long-term social strategies.

Identify key focus points for your brand to guide all your content creation.

Customizable, pre-populated links to your social media accounts and resources for easy access.

Easily track goals, target audience, content types, hashtags, and metrics to ensure your campaigns are optimized for success.
